Are the test questions official?
No. The questions on CivicLearn are educational simulations designed to reflect the spirit, difficulty, and main themes of the Australian citizenship test. They are not taken from any government source. Official test questions are not publicly released by Australian authorities.
What is the difference between the test modes?
CivicLearn offers several training modes adapted to different needs:
  • Test Simulation: Mirrors the official test format (20 questions, timer, results at the end).
  • Quick Test: 5 random questions, no time limit, instant correction.
  • Custom Test: You choose one or more topics. This mode is adaptive: questions repeat until all are answered correctly.
  • Common Traps: Targeted practice focusing on the most frequent mistakes, without a timer.

How is the Australian citizenship test simulation graded?
The simulation follows the official Australian citizenship test rules. To pass, you must meet all of the following conditions:

You must answer at least 75% of all questions correctly, and you must answer all questions on Australian values correctly.

This means that a single incorrect answer in the Australian values section results in a fail, even if your overall score is 75% or higher.

This rule reflects the real test used by the Department of Home Affairs and is intentionally enforced in the simulation.
